Overview:
This mini application allows users to submit a business profile and receive a risk score, decision recommendation, and a breakdown explaining the factors that influenced that score. The goal is to demonstrate how a fintech platform or financial institution might present automated underwriting insights while making it clear how and why a decision was reached.

The system uses a simplified, transparent rules-based scoring model to simulate signals commonly used in business risk evaluation. These include things like operational stability, financial strength, verification status, public records, and reputation indicators.

The resuls are rendered in a dashboard designed to resemble the kind of decision interface underwriting or operations teams would use when reviewing applications.

Key Features:
- Business profile intake form
- Automated underwriting score generation
- Risk classification and decision recommendation
- Explainability panel highlighting contributing risk factors
- Category-level risk breakdown visualizations
- Example business scenarios for quick demos

Tech Stack:
- React + Vite
- Node.js + Express
- TailwindCSS
- Recharts

This project was built as a weekend prototype to explore some of the product and engineering challenges involved in building transparent, explainable risk scoring tools for financial decision systems.
